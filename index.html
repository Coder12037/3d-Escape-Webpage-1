Who are the members on your team? Arav Pavan
What is it and what is it solving?
This project is 3D Escape, where you move around a 3D world trying to survive before the timer runs out.
It is solving nothing, I guess boredom.
How does your idea contribute to society/the community? Who is the target segment for your idea?)
This can help kids in the community to learn this new way of raycasting and have fun and play.
The target age is probably 7+ years old.


What tools (coding languages, platforms, the cloud, etc.) do you know that you can build your idea using?
We used scratch to make the game, with tons of blocks (I worked 8 hours in a day.)
How much have you completed implementing the idea? How much longer will it take to implement it?
Majority has been completed, we just would add sound effects and “collectables” (which I tried, but they ended up bugging out.)
It would take probably 2 days more to FULLY complete it, with the “collectables.”
What is your motivation in participating in this Hackathon?
The ways I can learn new coding techniques and that there’s a prize. I have the accomplishment of an “amazing game.”

How to play: In This Game, You Need To use the left and right arrow keys to change where you're looking and your perspective. W,A,S,D is to move around. Normal mode- survive 60 seconds without the ghost killing you and running out of health. Hard mode- survive 50 seconds but it is in low 20 resolution and rounded off. The apples make you get 50+ health. (hard mode no apples) That's how to win!
